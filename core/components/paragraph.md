## Properties

### Size

The relative size of the copy in relation to others within the collection.

* R1 (18dp)
* R2 (16dp)
* R3 (14dp)
* R4 (12dp)

### Alignment

How the copy is aligned within the screen...

* Left
* Center
* Right
